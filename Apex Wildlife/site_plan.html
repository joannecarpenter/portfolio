Apex Wildlife Service, LLC

Site Plan

Overview

Purpose

Apex Wildlife Service, LLC is licensed in Virginia to perform all your animal removal needs. We specialize not only in removing problematic animals from your residence, business or property but also offer full exclusion services.

Site Name

The site name for this website will be: https://www.apexwildlifeservice.com/. This domain was previously selected by the client. It is a great representation of the site content and purpose as it includes the business's name.

Audience and Scenarios

The primary audience for this site is potential clients. A secondary audience for consideration is current clients. These audience will be asking questions such as:

  • What kind of services does this business offer?
  • What area does this business cover?
  • How long has this business been in operation?
  • What do other people think about the services they have received from this business?
  • Who do I contact for more information or to schedule an appointment?

Typography

Heading Font: Open Sans, Regular 400

The heading font is professional and blends well with the font used on the business logo.

Paragraph Font: Montserrat, Regular 400

The paragraph font is wider than the headline font and still easily readable in small text. This sans serif font also continues the relaxed feel of this website. It provides enough variation from the headline font to promote separation without interrupting the website's reading flow.
